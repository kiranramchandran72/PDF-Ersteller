Operation Notes

18.11.2017 : DEBRIDEMENT OF LEFT FOREARM + PLATING BOTH BONES FOREARM.

 

Indication for surgery :

Grossly contaminated wound with coal particles with extensive soft tissue injury over extensor aspect of left forearm with avulsed tendons from musculotendinous origin.

Procedure :

Patient in supine position under left brachial plexus block after draping and painting of left upper limb under tourniquet control dorsum of forearm wound debrided.  There was gross contamination with coal particles.  Found all extensor tendons avulsed from musculotendinous sites with crushed muscles with contamination which were debrided.  All extensor tendons distally divided at the 1cm distal wrist.  Bony edges scooped.  Brachioradialis was found divided.  Edges of bone found contaminated which needed scooping and trimming of bones.  There was 3x 2 cm wound over volar aspect of distal forearm for which edges trimmed,found partial fraying of palmaris longus which was debrided.  Flexor tendons are intact.  Wound of about 12 x 5 cm resulted over dorsum of forearm with critical raw area needs soft tissue ( flap) cover on later date.  Arm wound debrided found 30 % biceps muscle divided.   Contamination present, debrided.  Thorough wash with hydrogen peroxide and betadine given and tourniquet released.  Hemostasis secured.

Ortho notes :

Patient received from plastic surgery team.  Tourniquet inflated.  All fracture end of radius freshened;  Distal  radius fragment dugged into middle fragment and held with hook reduction clamp and fixed with 6 holed 3.5 DCP with 3 proximal and 2 distal screw.  Then proximal and middle fragemnt.  Reduced adn held with hook reductionclamp and fixed with 8 holed 3.5 DCP with 3 proximal and 3 distal screws.  Then both fractured end of ulna freshened andcut iwth saw and held with hook reduction clamp after reduction and fixed with 7 holed 3.5 DCP plate with 3 proximal and 3 distal screw.  Wound wash given.  Flap procedure to be done later.  Sterile dressing pplied.  Above elbow slab applied.  After checking reduction under C arm.
